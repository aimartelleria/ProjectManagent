Welcome to EcoTrack MGEP

EcoTrack is a web app that helps MGEP students and staff record eco-friendly actions, earn points, and visualise their progress toward a more sustainable lifestyle.

  • Log actions such as recycling, cycling, saving energy, and more.
  • Gain points for every action and unlock new levels.
  • See your progress over time through charts.
  • Manage your profile and review your past actions.

Ready to get started? Create an account or sign in.
